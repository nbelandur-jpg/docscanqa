How it works

  1. Upload PDFs via the web app.
  2. OCR & Extract text and key fields (form tables, signature/date blocks).
  3. Validate against your master field list (required/optional, datatypes, ranges).
  4. Flag GDP issues: missing signatures, wrong dates, strikethroughs not initialed, blanks.
  5. Report generates a defect summary with page anchors and remediation hints.
  6. Export PDF/Word and archive the audit log.
