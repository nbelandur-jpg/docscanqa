Everything you need to clean up docs and move faster.

DocuScan QA is your AI-powered document assistant: scan in bulk, catch GDP issues, export clean reports, and spin up SOPs & Work Instructions using suggestive templates.

📥 Bulk Upload & OCR

Drag in PDFs, DOCX, and images. Optical character recognition reads text, stamps, and hand-filled fields.

🔎 GDP Error Detection

Flags missing signatures/dates, inconsistent formats, un-initialed strikethroughs, and blank mandatory fields.

🧩 Suggestive Templates

Auto-suggests SOP & Work Instruction skeletons based on doc type to help you draft quickly and consistently.

📝 Export Clean Reports

One-click export to Word/PDF with a tidy findings list, evidence snippets, and fix recommendations.

📚 Basic Audit Log

Tracks who uploaded, when checks ran, and what was exported — simple, readable, and shareable.

💻 No Validation Required

Operates like Excel/Docs — ideal for startups and pre-validation prep. Keep it lightweight and flexible.
